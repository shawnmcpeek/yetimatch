## Ad Strategy

### Ad Placement (FREE TIER ONLY)

**Approved Placements:**
1. **Banner ad after quiz results** (primary)
   - Shown after user sees their personality result
   - Non-intrusive, doesn't interrupt flow
   - Dismissible after 5 seconds

2. **Interstitial between quizzes** (optional)
   - Only when starting a new quiz
   - Maximum 1 per session
   - Skippable after 3 seconds

**Forbidden Placements:**
- ‚ùå During quiz questions (kills engagement)
- ‚ùå Before showing results (feels manipulative)
- ‚ùå Video ads (too aggressive for quiz apps)
- ‚ùå ANY ads in paid tiers (breaks trust)

**Ad Networks:**
- Primary: Google AdMob (best fill rates)
- Backup: Unity Ads or AppLovin
- Expected eCPM: $1-3 (varies by region)

### Revenue Expectations
- **Free user with ads:** $0.005-0.015 per user
- **Basic tier user:** $2.09 one-time
- **Premium tier user:** $3.49 one-time

**Breakeven:** 1 Basic tier user = ~200 free users with ads

## Pricing Psychology

### Why These Prices Work

**$2.99 for Basic:**
- ‚úÖ Impulse purchase territory (< $5)
- ‚úÖ Feels like "less than a coffee"
- ‚úÖ .99 ending = psychological discount
- ‚úÖ Low enough for teens/students
- ‚úÖ High enough to feel valuable (not 99¬¢)

**$4.99 for Premium:**
- ‚úÖ Still under $5 barrier
- ‚úÖ Premium positioning without being expensive
- ‚úÖ Good profit margin (~$3.49 after fees)
- ‚úÖ $2 upgrade from Basic feels reasonable
- ‚úÖ High enough to signal "extra value"

**Why One-Time vs Subscription:**
- ‚úÖ Users HATE subscriptions for simple apps
- ‚úÖ One-time = better reviews and sentiment
- ‚úÖ Lower mental barrier to purchase
- ‚úÖ Simpler to explain and market
- ‚úÖ Better for quiz apps (not ongoing service)
